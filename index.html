Frequently Asked Questions

What information do you need to provide a quote?
Product specification, quantity, destination country, and any compliance or certification requirements. The more detail, the faster we can quote.
Do you handle customs and export documentation?
Yes — freight forwarding, customs clearance, and export documentation are coordinated end to end as part of our logistics service.
Can I sell equipment or materials to GE International directly?
Yes. Select "Selling Machinery / Equipment" or the relevant category in the form and our procurement team will follow up with an evaluation.
What's your typical response time?
Standard inquiries receive a response within one business day. Urgent shipment issues are handled same-day via our urgent contact line.
Do you work with clients outside the United States?
Yes — we operate as a US-registered entity but serve buyers and sellers across 150+ countries.
